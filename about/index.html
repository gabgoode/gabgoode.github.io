Hi! I'm Gab, and thank you for walking into my life :)
I am working on 2 new compositions, titled "Galaxy" and "A Midsummer Night's Dream" respectively. Feel free to reach out if you are curious about my demos, and I would love feedback!
I am reading GEB at the moment. Absolutely love it and highly recommend!
I currently live and work in Cambridge, Massachusetts. I'm not sure where I will be next, but I love traveling around the world! You can follow my journey of discovery by subscribing to my podcast :P

I try not to have too many attachments, but I do love my little obsessions.
I think I only need to do two things in life: the most difficult thing that I could possibly do and the simplest thing that I could possibly do.
I will spend a lifetime doing something really really hard, which I have yet to figure out what that is.
I am living each day to the simplest yet fullest, listening to my heart, my mind, and the world around me. I also keep a journal to record my daily thoughts and sensual pleasures.
